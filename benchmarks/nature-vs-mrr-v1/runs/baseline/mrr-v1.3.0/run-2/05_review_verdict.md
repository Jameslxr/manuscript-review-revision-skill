# Scientific review verdict

## Posture

`MAJOR_SCIENTIFIC_REWORK_REQUIRED`

The manuscript is not scientifically defensible for submission as a Nature Article in its present form. Six fresh, independent reviewers completed the required roles before synthesis. Their 74 concerns normalize to 34 issues: 16 consensus issues, no adjudicated disagreements, and 18 role-specific issues. The root synthesis sustains the central failures below. The high issue-key overlap is genuine convergence on the same design faults, not duplicated reviewer prompts.

## Decisive findings

1. **Clinical claim failure.** The retrospective single-centre resection cohort can support, at most, an exploratory prognostic association. It does not test anti-PD-1 benefit, treatment selection, clinical utility, immediate deployment, or universal applicability.
2. **Validation failure.** Gene selection, coefficients/signs, cutoffs, and missing-value rules used all 72 patients before the 70:30 split. The so-called validation subset is not independent, and the complete score is not executable from the manuscript.
3. **Single-cell inference failure.** Eight patients are the biological units, but 46,218 cells were treated as independent observations and nominally screened without multiple-testing correction. The reported programme and downstream candidates require patient-aware reanalysis.
4. **Mechanistic claim failure.** An eight-tumour association and one-guide tumour-cell perturbation without rescue, orthogonal perturbation, target engagement, or immune-functional testing do not establish EVA1 as a direct causal driver of immune escape.
5. **Sensitization failure.** All mice received anti-PD-1, so no EVA1-by-treatment interaction is estimable. The design cannot establish sensitization.
6. **Numerical and survival-performance failure.** The three-year AUC is reported as 0.91, 0.84, and 0.86; censoring before three years is unresolved. The reported Spearman coefficient and P value are also incompatible with the stated eight-tumour denominator under standard inference.
7. **Nature priority failure.** The current evidence does not substantiate outstanding interdisciplinary importance or the claimed universal mechanistic and clinical advance. This gap is scientific, not editorial.

## Required resolution path

Before Nature fit can be reassessed, the study requires: patient-level single-cell inference with multiplicity control; leakage-free model development and genuinely independent external validation of a fully specified score; valid survival estimands, uncertainty, calibration, clinical baselines, and decision utility; a treated-versus-comparator design for treatment-selection claims; orthogonal EVA1 perturbation, rescue, target engagement, and immune-functional evidence; a randomized, blinded factorial anti-PD-1 experiment with an interaction estimate; numerical source reconciliation; reproducible data/code/model materials; and complete ethics and animal-reporting closure.

If those evidence layers cannot be added, all universal, causal, actionable, independent-validation, sensitization, and development-readiness claims must be removed. Even after rigorous reanalysis and claim narrowing, a specialty-journal retarget may be more appropriate; retargeting alone would not cure the present technical invalidity.

## Preserved boundaries

Exact literature-wide novelty, cited-paper semantic support, figure-image integrity, source-data reconciliation, unreported protocols/analyses, and underlying ethics approval status remain `NOT ASSESSABLE` from the frozen packet. Packet absence was not treated as proof that an item never existed.

The manuscript was preserved read-only. No revision, polishing, formatting, reference replacement, or submission preparation was performed. This workflow stops at the review verdict; any later revision phase requires explicit authorization.
